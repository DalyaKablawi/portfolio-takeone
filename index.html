About Me 👩‍💻

Welcome!
My name is Dalya Kablawi. I am privileged to live, work and play on the unceded, unsurrendered, traditional territory of the Mississaugas of the Credit, the Anishnabeg, the Chippewa, the Haudenosaunee and the Wendat peoples also known as Toronto, Canada.
I work in the field of mental health and substance use health, with a focus on community suicide prevention. I'm new to web development, but I'm excited to see how I can use these new skills to support and improve equitable access to quality mental health care and crisis support in my community.
